# Kelowna Wildlife Tracker

## Purpose

Kelowna Wildlife Tracker is a mobile-friendly application that allows residents of Kelowna, British Columbia to record wildlife sightings using GPS-enabled devices.

The goal is to make wildlife observation reporting easier, faster, and more accessible for everyday citizens while improving the quality and quantity of local biodiversity data.

Users can:

* Log wildlife sightings
* Automatically capture GPS coordinates
* Upload photos
* Record notes and habitat observations
* View sightings on a map
* Prepare information for submission to provincial wildlife databases

The application is designed to be simple enough for casual users while generating data that aligns with provincial wildlife reporting requirements.

## Primary Audience

* Kelowna residents
* Birders
* Naturalists
* Hikers
* Students
* Educators
* Citizen scientists

## Core Principle

Reduce friction between wildlife observation and wildlife reporting.
