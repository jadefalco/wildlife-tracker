# Potential Provincial Integration

The application is being designed to align with wildlife reporting requirements used by the Province of British Columbia.

Current provincial workflows rely on:

* Manual submission forms
* Citizen science platforms
* Professional environmental reporting

Future integration opportunities may include:

* API-based submission
* Data export formats accepted by the Province
* Integration with provincial biodiversity databases
* Integration with citizen science platforms

The application should be designed so that future integration is possible without major redesign.

Important:

Do not assume an API exists.

Future provincial integration must be validated through discussions with provincial contacts.
