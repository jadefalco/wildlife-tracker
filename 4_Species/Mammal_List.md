# Mammals

## Purpose

Common mammal species supported in Version 1 of Kelowna Wildlife Tracker.

These species are relevant to Kelowna and surrounding Okanagan habitats.

---

* Mule Deer

* Black Bear
* Grizzly Bear
* Raccoon
* Red Fox
* Bobcat
* Lynx
* Snowshoe Hare
* Nuttall's Cottontail
* Beaver
* Muskrat
* Chipmunk
* Ground Squirrel
* River Otter
