# Reptiles and Amphibians

## Purpose

Common reptile and amphibian species supported in Version 1 of Kelowna Wildlife Tracker.

These species are known to occur in Kelowna and the surrounding Okanagan Valley.

---

## Reptiles and Amphibians

### Turtles

* Painted Turtle
* Western Painted Turtle

### Frogs and Toads

* Western Toad
* Pacific Tree Frog

### Salamanders

* Long-toed Salamander

### Snakes

* Common Garter Snake
* Western Terrestrial Garter Snake
* Garter Snake
* Rubber Boa
* Racer (Yellow-bellied Racer)

---

## Future Expansion Candidates

Potential additions:

* Great Basin Gopher Snake
* Western Rattlesnake
* Tiger Salamander
* Columbia Spotted Frog

Future additions should be based on local species occurrence and stakeholder feedback.

## Data Quality

If a user is uncertain of species identification:

Allow selection of:

"Not Sure / Other"

Accurate observations are more valuable than incorrect identifications.
